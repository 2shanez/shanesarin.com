SaaS Services Agreement — TechCorp Inc.

Section 8.3 — Limitation of Liability

IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, INCLUDING WITHOUT LIMITATION, LOSS OF PROFITS, DATA, USE, GOODWILL, OR OTHER INTANGIBLE LOSSES, RESULTING FROM (I) YOUR ACCESS TO OR USE OF OR INABILITY TO ACCESS OR USE THE SERVICE; (II) ANY CONDUCT OR CONTENT OF ANY THIRD PARTY ON THE SERVICE...
⚠️
Third-Party Liability Gap

Section 8.3(ii) excludes liability for third-party conduct, which may leave your client exposed if vendor uses subcontractors. Consider adding carve-out for vendor's agents.

⚠️
Missing Cap on Direct Damages

No ceiling specified for direct damages. Industry standard is 12 months of fees. Recommend adding explicit cap language.

✓
Standard Consequential Damages Waiver

Mutual waiver of consequential damages is market-standard and protects both parties equally.
