How It Works

Truth as a protocol

Prism creates a decentralized network where trusted organizations compete to verify facts—with their reputation on the line.

1
📝

Claim Submitted

Anyone can submit a claim for verification. Breaking news, viral posts, political statements, scientific claims—anything that can be fact-checked.

2
🔍

Attesters Verify

Verified organizations—journalists, institutions, domain experts—investigate and stake their reputation on a verdict. TRUE, FALSE, or UNVERIFIABLE.

3
⛓️

Consensus Sealed

When enough trusted attesters agree, consensus forms. The verdict and all attestations are permanently recorded on-chain. Immutable forever.

Economics

Skin in the game

Aligned incentives create reliable truth. Attesters earn rewards for accuracy—and lose reputation for mistakes.

⭐

Reputation Staking

Every attestation stakes your reputation score. Get it right, your score rises. Get it wrong, it falls. Your reputation determines your influence on consensus.

💰

Verification Rewards

Accurate attesters earn PRISM tokens from the verification pool. Publishers and platforms pay for priority verification. Accuracy pays.

📉

Slashing for Errors

Consistently wrong attestations trigger reputation slashing. Severe or intentional errors can result in loss of attester status. Stakes are real.

⚡

Speed Bonuses

First accurate attesters on breaking news earn bonus rewards. Creates race to verify, not race to publish unverified.

🏆

Tier Advancement

High-performing attesters can advance tiers. More influence, more rewards, more responsibility. Meritocratic hierarchy.

🔗

Platform Licensing

Social networks and publishers pay to integrate Prism verification. Revenue flows to accurate attesters. Truth becomes profitable.
