Investor FAQ

The hard questions, answered

We've heard them all. Here's the honest answer.

Q: Isn't this just insurance? Why does it need blockchain?

Traditional insurance is adversarial—they profit by denying claims. Blockchain makes it trustless. When data triggers the contract, no one can stop the payout. Not us, not an adjustor, not anyone. That's not possible with traditional rails.

Q: What happens if Chainlink goes down?

Chainlink secures $75B+ in DeFi with 99.99% uptime. We use multiple oracle nodes with consensus—it's more reliable than any single API. But even in extreme scenarios, we have fallback data sources and refund guarantees.

Q: How do you handle adverse selection and fraud?

Parametric insurance is fraud-resistant by design. We don't pay based on what you claim—we pay based on what happened. Flight 2 hours late? Oracle verifies it. Rain ruined your beach day? Weather station confirms. No room for fraud.

Q: Why can't Lemonade/Root/traditional insurers do this?

They're trying, but they're building on legacy rails. Their "instant" claims still need human approval. They can't do truly automatic payouts—their systems require a decision step. Smart contracts execute; they don't decide.

Q: What's the TAM and path to $1B?

$90B travel insurance growing 15% CAGR. We start consumer, expand to B2B (airlines, OTAs want this as a feature). 5% of the travel insurance market = $4.5B. Then apply the model to weather, events, deliveries—each is a multi-billion dollar vertical.
