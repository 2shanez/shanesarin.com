The Thesis

Every company will need to track, report, and reduce emissions. EU CSRD, SEC climate rules, California SB 253, customer pressure—the regulatory tsunami is here. But the tools suck: spreadsheets, consultants, and prayers.

CarbonOS is the operating system for corporate climate compliance.

The Problem

Carbon accounting today is broken:

📊 Spreadsheet Hell

Data scattered across dozens of Excel files. Manual calculations. Formula errors. “Which emissions_final_v3_FINAL.xlsx is the right one?”

💸 $300k Consultants

Big 4 firms charge a fortune for carbon audits. Small changes? Another engagement. Questions? Bill by the hour.

🔗 Supply Chain Blindness

90% of emissions are Scope 3 (supply chain). Good luck getting data from 500+ suppliers manually.

⏰ Compliance Whiplash

CSRD, SEC, CDP, GRI, TCFD… Different frameworks, different formats, different deadlines. Same data, repackaged endlessly.
