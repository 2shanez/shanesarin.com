# Muse – The AI Studio for Creators

**Muse** is the unified AI platform where creators ideate, create, and monetize—all in one place. The Adobe Creative Cloud of AI.

## The Thesis

Creators need AI tools but they're scattered:
- ChatGPT for writing
- Midjourney for images
- CapCut for video
- Runway for effects
- Descript for audio

**The problem:** 8+ tools, $200+/month, endless context switching, no unified workflow.

**The solution:** One AI-native studio for everything creators need.
