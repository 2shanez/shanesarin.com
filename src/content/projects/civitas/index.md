## The $80 Billion Problem

Americans leave **$60-80 billion** in benefits unclaimed every year. Not because they don't qualify—because the system is designed to make them fail.

- **1,300+ federal programs** across dozens of agencies
- **Thousands more** at state and local levels
- Average application takes **23 hours** across fragmented systems
- Government is *reactive*: you must find programs, not the other way around

## The Estonia Model

Estonia has 99% of government services online. But more importantly, they have **proactive government**:

- Government contacts *you* when you're eligible for something
- X-Road data exchange means agencies share information seamlessly
- Once-only policy: never asked for the same data twice

**The US has none of this infrastructure.**

## Civitas: Flip the Model

Instead of citizens navigating agencies, we flip the model entirely.

### 🎯 Proactive Eligibility Engine
Continuously matches citizens against ALL available programs across federal, state, and local levels. You don't search—benefits find you.

### 🔄 Cross-Agency Orchestration
One request flows seamlessly across SSA, IRS, HHS, state agencies. No more filling out the same information 47 times.

### ⚡ 23 Hours → 5 Minutes
Auto-fill applications using verified data. What used to take weeks happens in a single session.

### 🤖 Conversational Transactions
AI that actually *completes* applications for you—not chatbots that link to PDFs.
