# Prism – The Trust Layer for the AI Age

**Prism** is building trust infrastructure for the internet—and proving it works with a killer app.

## The Two-Layer Play

### Layer 1: Infrastructure (B2B)
The "Stripe for verification" that every platform needs:

- **Provenance API**: Cryptographic proof of content origin (human vs AI, which model, when created)
- **Verification SDK**: Drop-in verification for any platform with 99.7% accuracy
- **Compliance Dashboard**: EU AI Act automation, audit trails, multi-jurisdiction support

### Layer 2: Prism News (Consumer)
The first news platform where truth is cryptographically verified:

- **Reputation-staked attesters**: Organizations (Reuters, BBC, AP) stake their reputation on verdicts
- **Trust scores**: See exactly who verified what and why
- **On-chain records**: Every verification sealed on blockchain forever
- **No single arbiter**: Consensus of trusted sources, not centralized decree

## The Problem We Solve

**For Platforms & Enterprises:**
- Can't distinguish AI content from human content
- EU AI Act mandates labeling (fines up to €35M)
- No audit trail for content provenance
- Training data liability (NYT vs OpenAI)

**For News & Truth:**
- Fact-checking is too slow (days after viral spread)
- Centralized arbiters with no accountability
- No skin in the game for accuracy
- Misinformation spreads 6x faster than truth

| Tier | Type | Examples | Reputation |
|------|------|----------|------------|

| Tier 2 | Domain Experts | Harvard, WHO, Nature | 90-97 |
| Tier 3 | Fact-Checkers | Snopes, PolitiFact | 85-95 |
| Tier 4 | Community | Verified individuals | Earned |
