## The Problem

Law firms bill $500/hour but run on email, Word docs, and manual research. Associates spend **60% of their time** on tasks AI can do in minutes:

- **8+ hours** for comprehensive case research
- **Thousands of pages** reviewed manually in discovery  
- **$150K+/year** per associate on repeatable tasks
- **Missed precedents** because nobody can read everything

## The Insight

Legal work isn't creative—it's pattern matching at scale. Research, contract review, discovery: these are workflows, not artistry. The best lawyers aren't doing more manual labor; they're applying judgment to the right problems.

**What if AI handled the 60% so lawyers could focus on the 40% that matters?**

## CaseBrief: The AI Associate

An AI-powered platform that automates legal workflows end-to-end:

### 🔍 Legal Research at Scale
Search across every federal and state case, statute, and regulation. CaseBrief finds relevant precedents, analyzes holdings, and cites sources—in seconds. Includes automatic Shepardizing and adverse authority detection.

### 📄 Contract Analysis
Upload any contract and get instant issue-spotting, risk scoring, and clause-by-clause analysis against your firm's playbook. Flags missing protections, non-standard terms, and hidden liabilities.

### 📋 Discovery Automation
Review thousands of documents in minutes. AI-powered privilege detection, relevance scoring, and automatic categorization. No more armies of contract attorneys.

### ✍️ Brief & Motion Drafting
Generate first drafts with proper citations and Bluebook formatting. Train on your firm's winning arguments and adapt to judge-specific preferences.
